# Features Overview

Isaac is a comprehensive farm management system. Here's everything it can do:

## Dashboard
- Real-time weather display with current conditions
- Daily task summary and overdue alerts
- Quick statistics (plants, animals, tasks)
- Cold protection warnings
- Animal feed schedules
- Storage monitoring
- Bible verse of the day

[Learn more about the Dashboard](Dashboard.md)

## Plant Management
- Complete plant database with growing requirements
- Care logging (watering, fertilizing, pruning)
- Automated care reminders
- Frost sensitivity tracking
- Harvest recording
- Import plants from reference databases (PFAF, Permapeople)
- Tag system for organization

[Learn more about Plant Management](Plant-Management.md)

## Animal Management
- Pets and livestock tracking
- Health records and care schedules
- Worming, vaccination, hoof trim, dental tracking
- Cold sensitivity and blanket alerts
- Feed management
- Expense tracking
- Livestock production/slaughter tracking

[Learn more about Animal Management](Animal-Management.md)

## Task & Calendar
- Three views: Day, Week (default), Month
- Events and reminders (todos) on calendar
- Visual distinction between event types
- CalDAV sync with external calendars
- Recurring tasks
- Priority levels
- Weather-dependent tasks

[Learn more about Tasks & Calendar](Tasks-and-Calendar.md)

## Maintenance Tracking
- **Vehicles**: Cars, trucks, tractors, ATVs
  - Mileage and hour tracking
  - Service schedules (oil, tires, brakes, etc.)
  - Maintenance history and costs

- **Equipment**: Tools, implements, machinery
  - Hour tracking
  - Maintenance schedules
  - Service history

- **Home**: House and structure maintenance
  - Recurring maintenance tasks
  - Completion logging
  - Cost tracking

- **Farm Areas**: Fields, pastures, gardens
  - Area size and soil tracking
  - Maintenance schedules
  - Plant/animal assignments

[Learn more about Maintenance Tracking](Maintenance-Tracking.md)

## Weather Integration
- Ambient Weather station integration
- National Weather Service forecasts
- Automatic alerts for:
  - Frost warnings
  - Freeze warnings
  - Heat warnings
  - Wind warnings
  - Rain warnings
- Configurable thresholds
- Cold protection recommendations

[Learn more about Weather Integration](Weather-Integration.md)

## Production Tracking
- Plant harvest recording with quantity/quality
- Livestock processing records
- Weight tracking (live, hanging, final)
- Cost-per-pound calculations
- Production history

## Seed Catalog
- Seed inventory management
- Planting information (depth, spacing, timing)
- Growing requirements
- Germination and maturity tracking
- Category organization

## User Management
- Multi-user support
- Role-based permissions (Admin, Editor, Viewer)
- Custom roles
- Kiosk mode for shared displays
- Session management

[Learn more about User Management](User-Management.md)

## Integrations
- **Weather**: Ambient Weather Network, NWS
- **Calendar**: CalDAV (iCloud, Nextcloud, Radicale, Proton)
- **Email**: SMTP notifications
- **Plant Data**: PFAF, Permapeople import

[Learn more about Integrations](Integrations.md)
